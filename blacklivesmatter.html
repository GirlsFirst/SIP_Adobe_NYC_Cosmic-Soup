Amelia A J Foy



As a white person, I grew up in a society that made race a non-issue for me.So i assumed for most of my childhood the same could be said for everyone else. But especially as a Londoner, it became apparent that was not the case for many people around me. This spread is for my friends who are POC, but specifically for my black friends, their families and their community. Racism is international, structural, and deadly. I don't agree with #AllLivesMatter because clearly,if black people are being killed on the daily, they do not matter. Not to the judicial system, not to the government, not to those with the power to execute CHANGE. I stand in solidarity with #BlackLivesMatter.


Amelia A J Foy, deep-and-artsy.tumblr.com
