From Choreolanus on tumblr



I wanted to create this piece after being monumentally moved by Lin Manuel Miranda's Tony acceptance speech in the form of a sonnet dedicated to the tragedy at the Pulse Club in Orlando. As a member of the LGBTQ+ community myself, his words meant a lot to me, especially the bit about how "love is love is love...it cannot be killed or swept aside" This piece was my 'thank you' to Lin, as well as my contribution to try and help heal the community.


Choreolanus, tumblr

In memory of our lost Latinx queer siblings. Their names are the ones we should remember. You have not died in vain. You will not die from our mouths, minds or hearts. We love you. To my living queer siblings - I love you, value you, and I am so happy you are here.
